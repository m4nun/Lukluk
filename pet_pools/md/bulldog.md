# Bulldog

*{id}: bulldog | species: dog | breed_or_category: Bulldog}*

Laid-back, courageous, and affectionate. Bulldogs are the ultimate couch companion — low-energy, loyal, and endlessly charming despite their grumpy faces.

## 🧠 MBTI Personality

**ISTP — The Relaxed Guardian**

Bulldogs are the introverts of the dog world. They value comfort above all else, form strong bonds with their family, and approach life at their own deliberate pace. They are stubborn but deeply affectionate.

## 🤖 Agent Guidance

**Who Is This Pet For?**

Perfect for apartment dwellers, low-energy households, and people who want a calm, loyal companion without intense exercise demands. Ideal for retirees and homebodies. Great with children (patient and sturdy).

**Hidden Costs**

Pet insurance or substantial medical savings (essential), air conditioning costs, special cleaning wipes for skin folds, special bowls for flat-faced dogs.

**Common Mistakes**

Not budgeting for medical costs. Exercising in heat. Overfeeding (they gain weight easily). Not cleaning skin folds regularly. Buying from irresponsible breeders.

**Adoption Advice**

Find a breeder who screens for brachycephalic and joint issues. Bulldog rescues exist but dogs often have pre-existing health conditions. Expect higher-than-average lifetime medical costs regardless of source.

**Decision Factors**

Can you afford potentially high medical bills? Do you have air conditioning? Are you okay with a shorter companionship (8-10 years)? Can you commit to regular skin fold cleaning?
