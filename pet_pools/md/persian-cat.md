# Persian Cat

*{id}: persian-cat | species: cat | breed_or_category: Persian}*

Calm, dignified, and luxurious. Persian cats are the aristocracy of the cat world — quiet, gentle, and perfectly content lounging in sunbeams.

## 🧠 MBTI Personality

**ISFP — The Gentle Artist**

Persian cats are the introverts who prefer peaceful environments and gentle attention. They are not demanding but appreciate quiet companionship. They thrive on routine and predictable, calm households.

## 🤖 Agent Guidance

**Who Is This Pet For?**

Ideal for calm households, apartment dwellers, and people who want a low-energy, quiet companion. Perfect for people who enjoy grooming rituals and have time for daily coat care. Great for retirees and quiet professionals.

**Hidden Costs**

Professional grooming every 4-6 weeks, special eye cleaning products, air conditioning (they overheat easily), elevated food bowls for flat-faced cats.

**Common Mistakes**

Underestimating the daily grooming commitment. Not screening breeders for PKD. Assuming all cats are low-maintenance — Persians require significant daily care.

**Adoption Advice**

Many Persian cats are in breed-specific rescue. Insist on PKD screening from breeders. Look for "doll-face" Persians if you're concerned about extreme brachycephalic features.

**Decision Factors**

Can you commit to daily brushing? Are you willing to clean eyes daily? Do you have budget for professional grooming? Can you keep your home cool?
