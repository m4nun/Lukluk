# Golden Retriever

*{id}: golden-retriever | species: dog | breed_or_category: Golden Retriever}*

Friendly, devoted, and intelligent. Golden Retrievers are the classic family companion — eager to please, great with children, and endlessly enthusiastic.

## 🧠 MBTI Personality

**ESFJ — The Enthusiastic Companion**

Golden Retrievers are the extroverts of the dog world. They thrive on social interaction, bond deeply with their families, and approach life with joyful enthusiasm. They are people-pleasers who need to be part of family activities.

## 🤖 Agent Guidance

**Who Is This Pet For?**

Ideal for active families, first-time dog owners, and people who want a social, affectionate companion. Great for homes with children and other pets. Best for people who have time for daily exercise and don't mind regular grooming.

**Hidden Costs**

Professional grooming, pet insurance (strongly recommended due to breed health risks), replacement toys (they chew enthusiastically), doggy daycare if you work long hours.

**Common Mistakes**

Underestimating the exercise needs — a bored Golden becomes destructive. Not budgeting for health issues. Letting them get overweight (they love food). Not socializing them early enough.

**Adoption Advice**

Look for reputable breeders who screen for hip/elbow dysplasia and eye conditions. Many Golden Retrievers are available through rescue organizations. Always meet the parents if possible.

**Decision Factors**

Can you commit to 45-90 minutes of daily exercise? Do you have budget for potential health costs? Are you okay with constant shedding? Is someone home most of the day?
