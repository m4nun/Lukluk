# Siamese Cat

*{id}: siamese-cat | species: cat | breed_or_category: Siamese}*

Elegant, vocal, and intensely social. Siamese cats form deep bonds with their humans and demand to be part of every conversation.

## 🧠 MBTI Personality

**ENFJ — The Charismatic Companion**

Siamese cats are the extroverts of the feline world. They talk constantly, follow you everywhere, and treat your home as their stage. They need companionship and mental stimulation — a lonely Siamese is a loud, unhappy Siamese.

## 🤖 Agent Guidance

**Who Is This Pet For?**

Perfect for people who want an interactive, talkative companion. Ideal for homes where someone is around often — retirees, work-from-home professionals, or families with older children. Great for cat lovers who want a "dog-like" cat.

**Hidden Costs**

Cat trees and climbing furniture (they need vertical space), puzzle toys for mental stimulation, potential dental care costs, a second cat as companion if you're away often.

**Common Mistakes**

Getting a Siamese when you want a quiet, independent cat. Leaving them alone for long hours. Not providing enough vertical space and enrichment. Underestimating their intelligence — they learn to open doors and cabinets.

**Adoption Advice**

Many Siamese cats and mixes are in rescue. Purebred Siamese from breeders should have health screening for eye and dental issues. The traditional "apple-head" Siamese tends to be quieter than the modern "wedge-head" type.

**Decision Factors**

Can you handle constant vocalization? Is someone home most of the day? Do you have patience for a demanding, intelligent companion? Are your walls thick enough for apartment living?
