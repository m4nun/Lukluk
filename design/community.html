Hot take after 3 years with a Siamese: they're not "difficult" — they're communicative. Once you learn their vocal patterns, you realize they're just telling you exactly what they want. Mine has distinct meows for "food," "play," "clean my litter," and "you're 5 minutes late for lap time." It's honestly easier than a cat that just silently resents you 😂

Anyone else decode their cat's vocabulary?
